About Masee's

Masee’s A Fashion Footwear & Accessories shop located in Ambur - The Leather city of India.

The Leather products made here are rooted with European designs and crafted using high quality leather. Making use of this availability, Masee’s gives its customer’s unique, differentiated and exclusive international brands at affordable and concession prices.

Masee’s is more than just the sum of its products – it’s a shopping experience that promises to surprise, amaze and amuse its customers by delivering extraordinary customer experience.

With wide verities of brands offering all kinds of shoes, Right from formal lace up, easy slip - on, boots, Brogues, sneakers, loafers, flip flops, sandals and fabulous range of bags and accessories. There is always something special for every occasion in store.

Here we provide a platform for our customers to buy our complete range of products online right from their place. We door deliver our products within India.
